Privacy Policy

At EvaTrue, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us when placing an order or contacting us.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us through WhatsApp, our website, or our social media pages, we may collect basic information such as:

• your name
• your phone number
• your delivery address
• details related to your order

This information is only collected when you voluntarily provide it to us.

How Your Information Is Used

The information you provide is used only to:

• process and confirm your order
• arrange delivery
• communicate with you about your purchase
• provide customer support if needed

Information Sharing

EvaTrue does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your information will only be used for the purpose of completing your order and providing customer service.

Communication & Privacy

Customers may interact with EvaTrue through platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok when viewing products or placing orders. Communication related to orders typically takes place through WhatsApp or other direct messaging platforms.

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information you provide to us and use it only for the purpose of processing orders and assisting customers.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through WhatsApp and we will be happy to assist you.
